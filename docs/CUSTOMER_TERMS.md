## 1. Service Overview

Career Systems OS helps early-career professionals and students present a clear, recruiter-ready digital profile. We convert your background, skills, and goals into structured deliverables—portfolio site, resume, LinkedIn alignment, and related assets—using a defined delivery process.

**Our process:**

1. **Purchase** — You select a package and complete payment.  
2. **Intake** — You submit accurate career information through our structured intake form.  
3. **Analysis & build** — We audit your materials, select the right approach, and produce your deliverables.  
4. **Quality review** — We verify links, formatting, and consistency before you see final work.  
5. **Your review** — You provide feedback within the revision limits for your package.  
6. **Delivery** — We hand off live URLs, files, and guidance documents.

We deliver **career positioning services**, not job placement. We do not guarantee interviews, offers, or employment outcomes.

**Purpose:** Full career positioning with strategy and premium support.

**Best for:** Competitive roles, career pivots, or candidates who want hands-on guidance.

**Includes everything in Launch, plus:**

- 30-minute strategy session (scheduled after initial deliverables are ready)

## 3. Delivery Timelines

### When the clock starts

Your **delivery timeline begins only after you submit a complete, validated intake form**. Payment alone does not start the delivery clock.

You should submit intake within **7 calendar days** of purchase. If intake is not received within **14 calendar days**, we may contact you to confirm whether you wish to continue. Extended delays may affect your project timeline or eligibility for service.

### Target delivery windows (business days)

Business days are Monday–Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays. The clock starts the first business day after your intake is accepted.

| Package | Target delivery | Maximum delivery |
|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| Foundation | 5 business days | 10 business days |
| Launch | 7 business days | 14 business days |
| Accelerator | 10 business days | 21 business days |

Target dates are goals, not guarantees. If we expect to exceed the target window, we will notify you.

### When the timeline pauses

The delivery clock **pauses** when we are waiting on you, including:

- Incomplete or inaccurate intake requiring resubmission  
- Missing required links (GitHub, LinkedIn where applicable)  
- No response to review feedback within **5 business days**  
- Failure to attend or reschedule a strategy session (Accelerator) within **14 days** of our scheduling offer  

The clock **resumes** when we receive the required information or feedback.

## 5. Revision Policy

### Included revision rounds

A **revision round** is one cycle of consolidated feedback from you, followed by our updates to included deliverables.

| Package | Included rounds | Scope |
|---------|-----------------|-------|
| Foundation | 1 | Portfolio copy and layout |
| Launch | 2 | Portfolio, resume, and LinkedIn copy |
| Accelerator | 3 | All included deliverables and narrative document |

### Revision vs. new work

**Included as a revision:**

- Fixing typos, wording, or formatting  
- Adjusting emphasis within the same target role  
- Layout or section-order changes within the agreed template  
- Aligning resume and portfolio language (Launch and Accelerator)

**Considered new work (not included; may require a new purchase or quoted fee):**

- Changing target role or industry focus after build has started  
- Adding new projects or experience not included in your original intake  
- Requesting a different template or full redesign after approval  
- Additional revision rounds beyond your package limit  
- Ongoing maintenance, job application services, or interview coaching  

We will notify you if a request falls outside your included revisions before performing additional work.

## 6. Refund Policy

All prices are in USD. Payment processing fees charged by our payment provider may be non-refundable.

### Before work begins

**Eligible:** Full refund minus payment processing fees.

**Conditions:**

- You request a refund **before** we begin analysis (typically within 48 hours of accepted intake, or before any deliverable work has started).  
- You have not yet received draft deliverables.

### After analysis begins

**Eligible:** Partial refund at our discretion, or credit toward a future package.

**Conditions:**

- We have started analysis, auditing profiles, or selecting templates.  
- Refunds are not available once build-phase work (portfolio or resume drafting) has begun.

We will confirm your project status if you request a refund at this stage.

### After delivery

**Not eligible for refund** once:

- Final deliverables have been handed off (live URL, files, and documentation sent), and  
- You have confirmed acceptance **or** **14 calendar days** have passed since delivery without a written dispute.

If deliverables materially fail to match your purchased package scope, contact us within **7 calendar days** of delivery. We will work in good faith to resolve the issue through corrections under your revision policy.

### Other situations

- **Chargebacks:** If you initiate a chargeback without contacting us first, we may suspend service and decline future engagements.  
- **Abandoned intake:** If intake is not completed within 14 calendar days of purchase and you do not respond to reminders, we may issue a partial refund minus an administrative fee, at our discretion.
