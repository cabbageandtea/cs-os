# Ideal client profile (ICP) — technical entry wedge

**OPS + acquisition reference.** Not for public pages.

---

## Who we serve

**Technical entry hiring** — candidates who need **proof-of-work online** and **aligned resume/LinkedIn** for software, data, analytics, or adjacent technical roles.

| Segment | Examples | Typical package |
|---------|----------|-----------------|
| Students / new grads | CS senior, bootcamp grad entering first role | Launch |
| Career changers into tech | Accountant → data analyst; retail → SWE | Launch or Accelerator |
| Active search, thin web presence | Has projects/repos, nothing deployed or aligned | Foundation → upsell Launch |

---

## Who to pursue for clients #2–#3

Prioritize same wedge as Client #1 showcase:

1. **Bootcamp grad** finishing program — needs portfolio + resume fast
2. **Career changer** with 1–2 portfolio projects — needs pivot narrative (Accelerator if budget allows)
3. **Student** in technical major — word-of-mouth on campus (your showcase becomes proof)

**Acquisition channels:** Personal network, LinkedIn DMs, campus/discord, contact form leads on `/contact`.

## Qualification (30-second check)

- [ ] Target role is **technical or technical-adjacent**
- [ ] Has **or will create** 2+ describable projects
- [ ] Will own **GitHub + email** (LinkedIn for Launch+)
- [ ] Understands **one-time package**, not ongoing coaching
- [ ] Not shopping for cheapest resume rewrite on Fiverr
