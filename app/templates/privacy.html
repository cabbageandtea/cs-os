{{ legal_entity }} (“Company,” “we,” “us”) operates {{ site_name }} at {{ site_domain }}. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information when you visit our site, contact us, purchase a package, or submit intake.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact inquiries: name, email, target role, status, and package interest you submit on our contact form.
  • Purchases: payment is processed by Stripe. We receive confirmation metadata (e.g., email, amount, package) — not full card numbers.
  • Intake and delivery: career information you provide (education, projects, work history, skills, URLs, goals, notes, and attestations).
  • Technical data: standard server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) for security and operations.
  • Operator sessions: a secure HTTP-only cookie for authorized staff on private admin routes only — not used for public marketing tracking.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Service is intended for adults and students managing their own career presence.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, deliver, and support packages you purchase.
  • Respond to contact and support requests.
  • Send transactional messages (e.g., intake links, delivery updates) via email providers you configure us to use.
  • Prevent fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and comply with law.
  • Improve our delivery process in aggregate without selling your personal information.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate (payment processing, email delivery, hosting). They process data under our instructions and their own privacy terms.
  • Platforms you authorize when you ask us to deploy to your GitHub or related accounts.
  • Legal and safety when required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, and integrity of the Service.

4. Retention

We retain client and purchase records as needed to deliver the Service, handle support and refunds, and meet legal obligations. Contact form leads are retained while relevant to sales follow-up, then deleted or archived on a reasonable schedule. You may request deletion of non-essential data by emailing {{ legal_support_email }}; we may retain records required for tax, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures appropriate to the data we hold. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for safeguarding your account credentials on third-party platforms.

6. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain processing of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact {{ legal_support_email }}. We will verify requests before responding.

California residents: We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. You may request disclosure or deletion as described above.

EEA/UK users: Our lawful bases include contract performance, legitimate interests in operating the Service, and consent where you opt in (e.g., contact form). You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

7. International transfers

We operate from the United States. If you access the Service from elsewhere, your information may be processed in the U.S. and other countries where our providers operate.

8. Third-party links

Our site links to third-party sites (GitHub, LinkedIn, Stripe checkout, etc.). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.
