## Structure (one page for interns / early career)

1. **Name** · city/state or “Open to remote” · email · LinkedIn · portfolio URL  
2. **Education** — degree, school, grad date, GPA if ≥3.5, relevant coursework (1 line max)  
3. **Experience** — internships, research, leadership with metrics  
4. **Projects** — 2–3 strongest; mirror portfolio order  
5. **Skills** — grouped: Languages · Tools · (optional) Certs  

---

## Impact bullet formula

`[Action verb] + [what you built] + [metric/outcome] + [tool]`

**Example:** Built ETL pipeline ingesting 40k+ dining records; cut manual reporting time 40% (Python, SQLite).
